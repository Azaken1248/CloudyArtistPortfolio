# Asset and Motion Plan

## Visual Assets

The provided reference imagery should guide the overall tone and composition of the portfolio. The frontend should feel inspired by those boards without copying them directly.

## Image Handling Plan

- Use optimized image formats where possible.
- Keep gallery images responsive and lazy-loaded later in implementation.
- Make sure artwork crops preserve subject clarity on smaller screens.
- Maintain consistent aspect ratios where possible for a cleaner grid.

## Motion Plan

- Animate the initial hero and navigation lightly on page load.
- Reveal gallery items as they enter the viewport.
- Use subtle scale and fade effects rather than heavy motion.
- Keep primary interaction feedback tied to meaningful actions.

## Hover and Focus Behavior

- On desktop, artwork tiles can dim slightly and reveal titles on hover.
- On keyboard focus, the same information should be discoverable without relying on pointer behavior.
- On mobile, important text should be visible without hover.

## Future Asset Notes

- The typography reference should remain locked before implementation begins.
- The color palette should stay consistent across backgrounds, buttons, and accents.
- Any future iconography should stay minimal and supportive rather than decorative.
