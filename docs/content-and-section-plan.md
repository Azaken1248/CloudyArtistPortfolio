# Content and Section Plan

## Public Sections

- Hero / introduction
- Artwork gallery
- Commission status and tiers
- FAQ
- Terms of service
- Contact

## Content Hierarchy

- The hero should introduce the artist and the overall tone of the portfolio.
- The gallery should be the main visual focus.
- Commission information should be easy to scan quickly.
- Policy and contact content should be present but not overpower the artwork.

## Copy Style

- Keep the copy concise and friendly.
- Use elegant, confident language rather than sales-heavy phrasing.
- Let the visuals carry the emotional tone while text stays practical.

## Gallery Content Rules

- Prioritize strong thumbnails and clear titles.
- Keep categories simple and understandable.
- Present image metadata only when it helps the user choose or inquire.

## Commission Content Rules

- Show open or closed status clearly.
- Present tiers in a scannable order.
- Keep descriptions specific enough to reduce back-and-forth.

## Out of Scope

- Owner/admin editing workflows.
- Authentication screens.
- Backend CRUD details.
- Public blog or unrelated marketing pages.
